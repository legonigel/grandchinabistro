About the Grand China Bistro

Deep Fried Eggplant

The Grand China Bistro is Poquoson's authentic Chinese restaurant. Founded in 2012 by Son "Sonny" Jiang and his wife Ms. Kay, the restaurant brings an authentic Chinese feeling to this small town.


We serve only the best food, cooked to perfection from the freshest ingredients. Our hand selected meats and fresh vegetables help make our food exceptional. We always use home made suace mixtures and never include MSG in our food, for the most authentic taste.


We are located in the Wythe Creek Plaza Shopping Center in Poquoson, Virginia on the corner of Victory Boulevard/Little Florida Road and Wythe Creek. If you need help finding us, feel free to call us. You can also call and have us deliver to you.
